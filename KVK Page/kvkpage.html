Klaipeda State University of Applied Sciences

About KVK

KVK is one of the largest applied sciences universities in Lithuania. It is located in the beautiful port city of Klaipeda. The university has a strong focus on practical training and research, with many opportunities for students to gain hands-on experience in their chosen field.

Faculties

  1. Faculty of Business
  2. Faculty of Health Sciences
  3. Faculty of Technologies
